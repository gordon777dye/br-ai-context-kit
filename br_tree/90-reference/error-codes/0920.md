**Error:** 0920

**Description:** Not enough storage for expansion

**Details:** Not enough contiguous storage exists to expand the dictionary or line table. `Save` the program in `source`(in `.BRS` format) and reload; space for 50 new lines will now be allocated. Repeat this procedure as many times as is necessary.
