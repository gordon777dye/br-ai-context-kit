**Error:** 9002

**Description:** RPN stacks full

**Details:** The `RPN stacks` is full, too many concatenations or variable references have been made; there are to many nested functions. Break the expressions and statements into smaller, less complex pieces. Reduce the number of variables and nested functions. Increase the size of RPN stack in the `BRConfig.sys` file but remember that RPN stack space comes from user memory. 
:Use **`Status` Stacks** and find the repeating pattern.  It may be looping between two routines both calling the other over and over.
