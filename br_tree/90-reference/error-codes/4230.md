**Error:** 4230

**Description:** DEPRECATED ERROR, as of 4.30: `Operating System Error` 30

**Details:** Faulty device.Read fault; could not read from the device. Fix the faulty device. Usually this is caused by a bad diskette drive. If you program has no reason to be accessing the diskette drive, look for a different cause. Fix the faulty device.About 95% of the time it's due to a bad diskette or a bad diskette drive. Try a different diskette or shut down the computer and blow the diskette drive out with a can of air.
