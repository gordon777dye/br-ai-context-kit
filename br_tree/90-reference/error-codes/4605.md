**Error:** 4605

**Description:** Could not lock the OnQ "In Process" byte

**Details:** Could not lock the OnQ "In Process" byte. (OnQ is an obsolete but very well designed character based ad-hoc reporting system that has been fully replaced by the ODBC driver).
