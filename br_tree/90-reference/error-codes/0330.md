**Error:** 0330

**Description:** More than 10 `sessions`; Wrong window number; Trying to print to a window that is a GUI and hung.

**Details:** - More than 10 sessions
- Wrong window number
- Trying to print to a window that is a GUI and hung. # Stop the process of the hung window and restart BR
