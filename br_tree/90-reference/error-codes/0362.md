**Error:** 0362

**Description:** Improper Spec-A control referenced cannot be found on the `FORM`

**Details:** Improper Spec-A control referenced cannot be found on the FORM
:`Visual Basic` Error, A `control reference` cannot be found on the Form
- Calling the wrong Form.
- Control Reference not added to Form # Check your `OPEN` `Statement` to make sure you are calling the right Form/`executable`.
- Add the `control` to your Form.
