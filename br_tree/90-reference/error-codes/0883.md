**Error:** 0883

**Description:** Field help text too large

**Details:** - Field help text too large (too many lines or too long) to display, or text is over 1017 characters long.
- A single line of the field help is longer than the (`Session Cols`-3) characters
- Field help is too many lines long.  There is not enough available lines to display the Field help on any side of the active input field. #Shorten the field help text.
- Consider breaking it up into two line by inserting a \n
- Consider combining two or more lines by removing \n
