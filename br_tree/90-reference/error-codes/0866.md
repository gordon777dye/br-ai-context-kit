**Error:** 0866

**Description:** All fields protected

**Details:** All defined fields in an `INPUT FIELDS` or `RINPUT FIELDS` statement are protected; no data can be entered or changed. Remove the P (protect) attribute from at least one field or change the statement to `PRINT FIELDS`.
