**Error:** 9006

**Description:** Too many nested structures

**Details:** Too many nested `IF Statement IF`, `FOR`/`NEXT`, and `DO LOOP` structures. Reduce the total number of nested IF, FOR / NEXT, and DO / LOOP structures to 80 or less.
