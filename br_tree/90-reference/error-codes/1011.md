**Error:** 1011

**Description:** Illegal `immediate statement`

**Details:** Use of `statement` which does not qualify as an immediate statement. Statements without line numbers are immediate statements; Business Rules! attempts to execute them immediately. Certain statements such as `GOSUB` and `DEF` cannot be acted upon immediately, and are therefore valid only with a `line number` or `Line Label (program) line label`. Add the line label. line number, or use a valid immediate statement.
