**Error:** 1150

**Description:** Illegal change or deletion of active line

**Details:** Illegal change to or deletion of active line. Can occur when a change is made to the line that called an active user-defined function. Can also occur when a statement within a multiple-statement line has caused an error and then a change is made to another statement on the same line, or when a GOSUB is active and attempting to change the line that called the GOSUB. Continue the program or END it and change the line.
