When launching `Business Rules!` a '''Couldn't get lock offset.** messagebox with a caption of **Error''' is displayed.

A solution is to exit all copies of `Business Rules!` on the workstation and then re-enter.

This is probably due to an issue related to `wbserver.dat`.
