**Error:** 0813

**Description:** Field ATTRIBUTE [..] not defined in the BRCONFIG.SYS file.

**Details:** A Field ATTRIBUTE [..] was referenced in a FIELDS statement that was not defined in the BRCONFIG.SYS file.
