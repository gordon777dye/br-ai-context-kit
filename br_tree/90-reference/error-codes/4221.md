**Error:** 4221

**Description:** DEPRECATED ERROR, as of 4.30: `Operating System Error` 21

**Details:** Drive not ready, diskette door is open, or printer not on. Close diskette drives door, fix faulty disk/diskette, and turn on power to printer and place printer on-line
