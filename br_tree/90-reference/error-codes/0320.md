**Error:** 0320

**Description:** Duplicate Local or  `Library Functions

**Details:** Library Function` definition A `user-defined function` was defined by more than one `Def` statement or was identified in both Def and `Library` statements. Remove the extra definition or *Def* or *Def Library* statement.
