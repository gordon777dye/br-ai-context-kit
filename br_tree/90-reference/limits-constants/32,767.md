In versions of `Business Rules!` prior to `4.20` **32,767** is the `System Limits|maximum length` for strings and the maximum number of items allowed in numeric arrays.  As of 4.20, this was raised to `99,999,999`.  

`Option` 60 will limit BR! 4.2+ to 32,767 characters in an individual string (among other things)
