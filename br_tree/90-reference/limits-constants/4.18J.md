4.18J

`OPTION` 61 will prevent automatic mapping of drives that are referenced 
by the second parameter of a drive statement and will produce a regular 
4 digit BR error when any drive references are made to unmapped drives. 

An OPTION 64 controls the client server timeout duration.
e.g.    OPTION 64 20  ! wait 20 seconds before timing out CS connection

`SAVE` will now always provide a confirmation dialog before overwriting a file.
