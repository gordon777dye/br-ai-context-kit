In an `FMT` clause, the letter "R" is a character identifier that means flush right. See `FMT#FMT Syntax|FMT Syntax`.

The letter R is also used in the `CR` `:Category:Format Specifications|format specification` and as a CR `PIC#Insertion Characters|insertion character`.

R is also a `Attribute (Screen)#Monochrome Attributes|screen attribute`, meaning "reverse".
