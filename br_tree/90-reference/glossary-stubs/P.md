In an `FMT` clause, the letter "P" (in upper or lowercase) is a character identifier that means anything entered is protected. See `FMT#FMT Syntax|FMT Syntax`.

As a `screen attribute` the letter "P" denotes `Attribute (Screen)#Control Attributes|protected` field.

In an `Print Fields|Print`/`Input Fields` statement as a `Format_specifications|Format Spec`/`Field_Specs|Field Spec`, the letter "P" can be used to display a `Picture`
