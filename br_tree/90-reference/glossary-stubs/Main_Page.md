Welcome to the ****. This wiki is dedicated to the **`Business Rules!`** programming language (a.k.a. BR) and the software developers who use it. Business Rules is formerly known as Workstation Basic. Currently containing `Special:Statistics|` articles,  this growing wiki is a great place to find information about the Business Rules! programming language. Our goal is to provide Business Rules! developers a convenient source of reference as well as an easy-to-use platform for documentation development. 

We encourage members of the **[https://brulescorp.com/brforum.live/ Business Rules! community]** to contribute their own knowledge to the Wiki by messaging an editor. A list of editors can be found *[http://brwiki2.brulescorp.com/index.php?title=Special:ListUsers&group=editor here]*. **New user registration** is also performed by editors upon request. Registered users can create discussion pages and send email to other users.

This entire site can be viewed by guests (no login required), while editing content is restricted to the [http://brwiki2.brulescorp.com/index.php?title=Special:ListUsers&group=editor editors group]. Contact *support@brulescorp.com* if you wish to become an editor, or wish to know more about the [http://brforum.brulescorp.com Business Rules! community].

If you come here often, you may be interested in just `Special:Newpages|new pages` or `Special:Recentchanges|recent changes`.
