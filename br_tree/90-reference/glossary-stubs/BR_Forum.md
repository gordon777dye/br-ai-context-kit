The **BR Forum** (brforum.brulescorp.com) is the place to post your questions, answers and other discussions about the Business Rules! programming language.

To keep up to date with what is going on on the BR Forum, it is suggested that you subscribe to the BR Forum's RSS feed.


===New Users===
*Register
*Help using the BR forum

When you get there, please check out the "Using this forum" section for a few pointers to help you get started.

You must be registered in the new forum in order to post messages. To do so, go to brforum.brulescorp.com  and Register. New registrations must be approved by a moderator, so there could possibly be a slight delay before that takes place. Once you are registered, log in using the Log in link in the upper right corner and you're ready to go.



==Older Forum: the Maillist==
The older "BR_forum" maillist is still in wide spread use as a portal to the online forum, just remember that every email you send or receive here will be published.
